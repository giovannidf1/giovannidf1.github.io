Rolling Fury

A 3D arcade game where you control a sphere rolling along an infinite bridge. Avoid obstacles, collect power-ups and challenge yourself to get the highest score!

Key Features

🎮

Simple Controls

Move the sphere left and right by tapping the sides of the screen. Intuitive controls for smooth gameplay!

⚡

Risk Mechanics

Tap the sphere to activate special effects - it can become YELLOW (invincible) or RED (instant game over)!

💎

Unique Power-Ups

Diamonds for invincibility, turtles to slow down time, coins to unlock new spheres!

🎨

15+ Unique Spheres

Each sphere has different special abilities: agility, explosion resistance, coin multipliers and extra lives!

🏆

Stunning 3D Graphics

Uses RealityKit for an immersive visual experience with realistic physics and particle effects!

📈

Infinite Progression

The game gets faster and faster as you advance. Challenge yourself to get the highest score!

Game Experience

Immersive Gameplay

Control your sphere along the infinite bridge with stunning 3D graphics and realistic physics.

Multiple Spheres

Unlock and play with different colored spheres, each with unique abilities and characteristics!

Advanced Challenges

Face increasing difficulty as you progress through the endless track with higher scores!

How to Play

1

Control

Tap the sides of the screen to move the sphere left and right along the infinite bridge.

2

Avoid Obstacles

Dodge all obstacles you encounter along the path to survive as long as possible.

3

Collect Power-Ups

Gather diamonds, turtles and coins to gain advantages and unlock new spheres.

4

Risk and Win

Tap the sphere to activate special effects - you might become invincible or lose everything!
