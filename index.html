Ball Dash - Deluxe

The ultimate bubble shooting game! Match colors, destroy bubbles, and collect stars!

Features

Simple Gameplay

Shoot colorful balls to make them collide with bubbles of the same color. Break groups of 3 or more!

Challenging Levels

Advance through dozens of exciting levels with unique designs and increasing difficulty.

Interactive Map

Explore beautifully designed maps as you progress through the adventure.

How to Play

  • Shoot colored balls and match 3 or more to destroy them.
  • Strategically aim to hit bombs or special items for extra effects.
  • Complete levels by collecting stars or achieving objectives.
